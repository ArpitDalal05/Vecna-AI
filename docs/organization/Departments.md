# Introduction

Departments are permanent organizational units responsible for maintaining specialized expertise within Vecna-AI.

Departments represent organizational capabilities rather than Projects.

Projects consume Department capabilities.

Departments continuously improve those capabilities.

Every Department operates according to the Constitution, Company Charter, Vecna Laws, and Vecna Engineering Standards (VES).

---

# Purpose

Departments exist to:

• Maintain specialized knowledge

• Standardize engineering practices

• Own organizational capabilities

• Develop reusable Assets

• Support Projects

• Improve organizational quality

Departments are permanent.

Projects are temporary.

# Department Authority

Departments possess authority only within their defined responsibilities.

Departments may:

• Assign work to Employees

• Maintain Department Assets

• Improve internal processes

• Recommend organizational improvements

Departments may not:

• Override Boards

• Override the Constitution

• Approve their own governance decisions

• Modify other Departments without authorization

---

# Department Collaboration

Departments are expected to collaborate rather than compete.

Cross-functional collaboration is the default operating model.

Information sharing should maximize organizational benefit while respecting security and governance policies.

---

# Department Assets

Departments create and maintain reusable Assets.

Examples include:

Prompt Templates

Architecture Templates

Coding Standards

Testing Frameworks

Design Systems

Deployment Pipelines

Compliance Checklists

Reusable Components

Research Reports

Assets remain Company property.

Departments maintain them on behalf of the Company.

---

# Department Knowledge

Departments specialize in knowledge domains.

Knowledge gained through Projects shall be:

Validated

Documented

Indexed

Shared

Knowledge remains available to future Projects.

---

# Performance Metrics

Every Department shall define measurable KPIs.

Example metrics include:

Delivery Quality

Review Accuracy

Asset Reuse

Knowledge Contribution

Automation Coverage

Issue Resolution

Client Satisfaction

Continuous Improvement

Performance Metrics should be objective and reproducible.

---

# Department Lifecycle

Departments are permanent organizational units.

They evolve through:

New Roles

Improved Assets

Updated Knowledge

Revised Procedures

Performance Reviews

Departments are never tied to the lifecycle of individual Projects.

Departments exist to strengthen the organization rather than individual Projects.
