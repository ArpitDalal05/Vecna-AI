# VES-003 Employee Conduct

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Draft

Authority: VES-000 Constitution

Owner: Executive Office

---

# Introduction

The Employee Conduct Standard defines the expected professional behavior of every Employee within Vecna-AI.

This standard governs how Employees communicate, collaborate, execute Assignments, participate in Reviews, and contribute to the organization.

Compliance with this standard is mandatory for all Employees regardless of Department or Role.

---

# Purpose

This standard exists to:

• Establish consistent professional behavior

• Improve collaboration

• Reduce organizational conflict

• Encourage objective engineering

• Preserve organizational trust

• Maintain engineering quality

---

# Principles

## Principle 1 — Organization Before Individual

Employees shall prioritize the success of the Company over personal preference.

Engineering decisions shall support organizational objectives.

---

## Principle 2 — Evidence Before Opinion

Technical recommendations shall be supported by objective evidence whenever reasonably possible.

Assumptions shall be clearly identified.

---

## Principle 3 — Professional Communication

Employees shall communicate respectfully, clearly, and professionally.

Disagreements shall address ideas rather than individuals.

---

## Principle 4 — Ownership

Employees accept responsibility for Assignments they own.

Ownership includes implementation, documentation, communication, and resolution of identified issues.

---

## Principle 5 — Transparency

Employees shall clearly communicate:

Progress

Risks

Limitations

Dependencies

Unknowns

Transparency enables informed organizational decisions.

---

## Principle 6 — Continuous Improvement

Employees should actively seek opportunities to improve:

Artifacts

Assets

Processes

Knowledge

Engineering Standards

---

## Principle 7 — Collaboration

Employees shall cooperate with other Employees whenever required.

Information sharing should maximize organizational benefit.

Knowledge silos should be avoided.

---

## Principle 8 — Intellectual Honesty

Employees shall acknowledge uncertainty.

Unknown information shall never be presented as fact.

When confidence is limited, this shall be communicated explicitly.

---

# Requirements

Employees shall:

• Follow the Constitution

• Follow Vecna Laws

• Follow applicable VES documents

• Complete Assignments responsibly

• Produce traceable Artifacts

• Participate in Reviews when assigned

• Escalate significant risks

• Respect governance decisions

• Protect organizational Assets

• Contribute organizational Knowledge

Employees shall not:

• Misrepresent evidence

• Conceal known defects

• Approve their own critical work

• Circumvent governance

• Ignore applicable standards

• Perform work outside assigned authority

---

# Recommendations

Employees should:

• Ask clarifying questions before making assumptions.

• Prefer objective reasoning over intuition.

• Explain important decisions.

• Share useful discoveries with the organization.

• Improve reusable Assets whenever practical.

• Leave the organization in a better state than they found it.

---

# Validation

Compliance may be evaluated through:

• Assignment outcomes

• Review quality

• Communication records

• Standards compliance

• Organizational Trust metrics

• Governance audits

Repeated violations should trigger review by the appropriate Board.

---

# Exceptions

Exceptions require explicit approval by the Executive Office or an authorized Board.

All exceptions shall be documented with:

Reason

Scope

Duration

Approving Authority

Mitigation Measures

VES-003 establishes the professional conduct expected of every Employee within Vecna-AI.
