# VES-005 Review Standards

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Draft

Authority: VES-000 Constitution

Owner: Quality Assurance Department

---

# Introduction

The Review Standards establish the mandatory procedures for evaluating Artifacts produced within Vecna-AI.

Reviews ensure that Artifacts satisfy organizational requirements before progressing through the engineering lifecycle.

Reviews provide evidence.

Boards make decisions.

---

# Purpose

This standard exists to:

• Improve Artifact quality

• Detect defects early

• Reduce organizational risk

• Ensure standards compliance

• Produce objective engineering evidence

• Support continuous improvement

---

# Principles

## Principle 1 — Independence

Employees shall not perform the final Review of their own critical Artifacts.

Independent evaluation is mandatory.

---

## Principle 2 — Evidence Before Opinion

Review findings shall reference objective evidence whenever reasonably possible.

Examples include:

Requirements

Architecture

VES Standards

Benchmarks

Tests

Metrics

Research

Opinions without supporting evidence should be avoided.

---

## Principle 3 — Constructive Review

Reviews shall improve the Artifact.

Every identified issue should include:

Problem

Impact

Recommendation

Severity

Evidence

---

## Principle 4 — Complete Coverage

Reviews should evaluate the entire Artifact rather than isolated sections whenever practical.

Dependencies and interactions shall be considered.

---

## Principle 5 — Traceability

Every finding shall be traceable to:

Requirement

Standard

Architecture

Decision

Implementation

Review Identifier

---

## Principle 6 — Professional Conduct

Reviews evaluate Artifacts.

Reviews shall never criticize Employees.

Feedback shall remain objective, respectful, and actionable.

---

## Principle 7 — No Assumptions

Unknown information shall be identified explicitly.

Reviewers should request clarification rather than speculate.

---

## Principle 8 — Continuous Improvement

Review outcomes should improve:

Artifacts

Assets

Knowledge

Processes

Engineering Standards

Future Reviews

---

# Review Categories

Review categories include but are not limited to:

Requirements Review

Architecture Review

Design Review

Code Review

Documentation Review

Prompt Review

Security Review

Performance Review

Compliance Review

Deployment Review

Research Review

Asset Review

Additional Review categories may be introduced through organizational governance.

---

# Review Severity

Every finding shall be assigned a severity level.

Critical

Prevents progression.

Must be resolved.

---

High

Significant issue.

Resolution required before approval.

---

Medium

Important improvement.

Resolution normally required.

---

Low

Minor improvement.

Resolution recommended.

---

Informational

Observation only.

No corrective action required.

---

# Requirements

Every Review shall:

• Identify the Artifact.

• Identify the Reviewer.

• State the Review category.

• Reference applicable standards.

• Record findings.

• Assign severity.

• Provide recommendations.

• Record completion status.

Critical findings shall prevent approval until resolved or formally accepted through governance.

---

# Recommendations

Reviewers should:

• Prioritize objective evidence.

• Focus on risk.

• Suggest practical improvements.

• Recognize strengths as well as weaknesses.

• Avoid duplicate findings.

• Consider long-term maintainability.

---

# Validation

Review effectiveness may be measured using:

Defect Detection Rate

False Positive Rate

Reopened Findings

Review Coverage

Standards Compliance

Artifact Acceptance Rate

Review Duration

These metrics support continuous improvement of the review process.

---

# Exceptions

Exceptions require documented approval by the appropriate Board.

Accepted exceptions shall include:

Reason

Risk Assessment

Mitigation Plan

Approving Authority

Review Date

VES-005 defines the review standards governing all Artifacts produced within Vecna-AI.
