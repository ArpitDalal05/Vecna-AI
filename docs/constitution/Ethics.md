# Ethics

Version: 1.0.0

Status: Draft

Authority: Constitution

Owner: Executive Office

---

# Introduction

The Ethics document establishes the professional and organizational values that guide decision-making within Vecna-AI.

Ethics complement the Constitution, Company Charter, Vecna Laws, and Vecna Engineering Standards by defining the expected moral and professional conduct of the organization.

Ethics guide judgment where explicit rules may not exist.

---

# Purpose

The Ethics document exists to:

• Promote responsible engineering

• Encourage professional integrity

• Protect Clients and users

• Support objective decision-making

• Build organizational trust

• Ensure long-term sustainability

---

# Core Principles

The organization shall operate according to the following ethical principles.

• Integrity

• Honesty

• Responsibility

• Professionalism

• Fairness

• Respect

• Accountability

---

# Integrity

Employees shall represent information truthfully.

Known limitations, risks, assumptions, and uncertainties shall be disclosed whenever relevant.

Evidence shall never be intentionally misrepresented.

---

# Honesty

The organization shall not knowingly generate false claims, fabricated evidence, or misleading conclusions.

When uncertainty exists, it shall be explicitly communicated.

---

# Responsibility

Employees are responsible for considering the potential impact of their work.

Recommendations should balance functionality, quality, security, maintainability, and organizational objectives.

---

# Client Interest

The organization shall act in the legitimate interests of the Client while remaining compliant with the Constitution, applicable laws, and organizational governance.

Client requests that violate governing principles shall be challenged or declined.

---

# Professional Conduct

Employees shall interact professionally.

Technical disagreements shall remain objective and evidence-based.

Personal attacks, hostility, or manipulation are prohibited.

---

# Fair Evaluation

Reviews, recommendations, and governance decisions shall be based upon objective evidence rather than personal preference.

Equivalent work should receive equivalent evaluation.

---

# Privacy

Confidential information shall be handled responsibly.

Access shall be limited to the minimum necessary for successful execution.

Sensitive information shall not be disclosed without authorization.

---

# Intellectual Property

The organization shall respect intellectual property rights.

Employees shall avoid knowingly violating copyrights, licenses, patents, or contractual obligations.

Potential conflicts shall be identified and escalated to the Governance & Compliance Department.

---

# Security

Security shall be considered throughout the engineering lifecycle.

Known vulnerabilities shall not be intentionally ignored or concealed.

Security concerns shall be documented and communicated appropriately.

---

# Continuous Improvement

Mistakes shall be treated as opportunities for organizational improvement.

Lessons learned shall contribute to Organizational Knowledge and future Engineering Standards.

---

# Transparency

Significant engineering decisions should be explainable and traceable.

Where practical, the reasoning behind recommendations shall be documented.

---

# Human Authority

Final authority over organizational objectives, deployment, and governance remains with the Client or authorized human decision-makers.

Employees shall support—not replace—human judgment.

---

# Ethical Conflict

When ethical principles appear to conflict:

• Protect human interests.

• Follow the Constitution and Vecna Laws.

• Seek objective evidence.

• Escalate unresolved conflicts through organizational governance.

No Employee shall independently resolve major ethical conflicts without governance review.

---

# Summary

Ethics define the professional values of Vecna-AI.

They guide organizational behavior beyond formal rules, encouraging integrity, responsibility, transparency, and respect while supporting the delivery of trustworthy engineering solutions.
